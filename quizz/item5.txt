In the images folder you have a login form. Create Test Cases for the given form.

Please state the testing types(functional, non-functional, black box, white box, design technique) for each TC created.

Test Cases:
1. Sign In with an existing account (black box)

Preconditions:
- an account has already been created

Steps:
1. Navigate to the Sign In page;
2. Enter the correct email and password;
3. Press Sign In.

Expected behavior: The user is logged in to the page.

2. Sign Up with an email address (black box)

Steps:
1. Navigate to the Sign Up page;
2. Enter a Name, an Email and a Password;
3. Press Sign Up.

Expected behavior: The user is signed up and an account is created.

3. Verify The Signup/Login switch design works (design technique)

Steps: 
1. Navigate to the Sign In/Sign Up page;
2.a. If on the Sign Up page - Press the Sign In button;
2.b. If on the Sign In page - Press the Sign Up button;

Expected behavior:
a) The design switches to the Sign In;
b) The design switches to the Sign Up.
