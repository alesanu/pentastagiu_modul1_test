Please state what is black box testing? How about white box testing? You can also give an example.

Answer: 
When running white box testing, we have access to the code,to the internal stucture of the app and we know exactly what is happening in "backstage".
Example: unit testing;

When running black box techniques, we don't know what is happening in back end.
Examples: Boundary value analysis,Equivalence partitioning;
